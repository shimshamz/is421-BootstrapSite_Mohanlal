About

I was born and raised in South Africa. Ever since I can remember, I have always loved cars and growing up, they were the only thing that I was interested in.

My interest in photography arose from taking photos of exotic cars. I found myself wanting to improve my photos and with that I started to learn the art and appreciate all types of photography, not just automotive.

I hope that one day my photography takes me to many different places around the globe where I get to experience a variety of different cultures.
